What is this?

beans for bits is a an ownership-aware device that controls real-world assets using digital protocols. In other words, a vending machine.
It was inspired by the idea of proplets.

beans for bits is controlled by an Ethereum client. The control protocol is dynamic, so the vending machine can adjust to market demand. The contract governing the jellybean machine is publicly viewable on the blockchain, allowing customers to verify the code.

How do I get my beans?

Jellybeans are dispensed from the vending machine at Abra's office in Mountain View, California.
Non-employees are welcome to purchase beans to feed our engineers, like at the zoo.
